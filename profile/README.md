# LUC-AI4FM

**Loyola University of Chicago Department of Computer Science — AI4FM**

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for the AI For Formal Methods (AI4FM) research group within the **Department of Computer Science** at **Loyola University Chicago**. We're a community of researchers, students, and collaborators advancing *formal methods*, *rigorous system design*, and *reproducible research tools*. ([CS Department][1])

---

## Mission

Our mission is to:

* **Formal methods research** that bridges logic, mathematics, and real-world computing systems.
* **Foster collaborative projects** across Computer Science and related fields.
* **Build open tools, datasets, and workflows** for formal methods, AI/ML, and LLM research.
* **Support student research experiences** through accessible open-source projects. ([CS Research][2])

This organization hosts repositories supporting:

* **Core research tools and libraries**
* **Data pipelines and experimental frameworks**
* **Papers, parsers, and utilities**
* **Community and education resources**

Current public repositories include:

* `tla-dataset-pipeline` — Pipeline for aggragating and transforming TLA+ datasets
* `FormaLLM` — TLA+ prompting pipeline for open source and commercial LLM models
* `paper-parse` — Python utilities for PDF parsing
* `FormaLLM-Reverse` — FormaLLM Fork focused on reversing TLA+ to textual specification documentation

> **Note:** More repositories and experimental projects may be visible depending on your access level. ([LUC-AI4FM on GitHub][3])
